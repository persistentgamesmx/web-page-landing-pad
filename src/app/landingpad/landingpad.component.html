The goal is to lead the way in developing immersive and interactive mixed reality experiences that captivate users and push the limits of what's possible in entertainment, blending creativity and innovation to create dreamlike digital worlds.

The Works (game in development)

Step into the nostalgic world of "The Works," a virtual reality game set inside a closed soda factory. Your mission? Produce one last batch of sodas before the facility is destroyed forever. Navigate through various zones, repair machinery, and solve puzzles with physically accurate interactions like cable simulation, crane operation, and more.

Immerse yourself in the retro visual style inspired by early 3D games on consoles like the Nintendo 64 and PlayStation 1. Experience the mystery of the main character's identity and memory loss, guided by a company assistant through radio transmissions. Discover the truth behind the assistant's AI nature and the ultimate test of your problem-solving skills.

"The Works" offers a unique blend of innovative gameplay mechanics, nostalgic visuals, and an intriguing storyline that will keep you hooked from start to finish. Are you ready to take on the challenge?

A game engine for the new VR standard

An all-in-one game engine designed for mixed reality development offers a range of features to aid developers in creating immersive experiences and accelerating the development process. These features include:

  • Physically Based Rendering (PBR) textures optimized for VR
  • Intuitive controller and hand interactions
  • Automatic collider generator
  • Integrated chatbot
  • Physics engine optimized for VR with enhanced stability
  • Multiple techniques for softbody simulation
  • Hand auto-posing and object grabbing
  • Stable car controller
  • Physics-based animation
  • Mobile and cloud versions with WebXR export capabilities
